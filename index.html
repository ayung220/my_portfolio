Dimple (Delegation Simple)

About the project

The Dimple application was created to complete college projects application, created through collaboration between PKBI parties. This application aims to support the mechanism of incoming mail disposition and delegation of authority to individuals or groups in implementing activities for PKBI and its affiliated communities. The term "dimple" symbolizes the dimples that bring a smile to the face, representing success in combining delegation and simplicity. Like a dimple that adds charm to the face, Dimple beautifies and simplifies the process, bringing joy and ease at every step.
